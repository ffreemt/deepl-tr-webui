Quick tips
  • Double click a cell or press Enter in a cell to edit.
  • Press Enter or click another cell to exit the in-cell editor.
  • Press the SAVEEDIT button to effect change after editing.
  • To turn on debug: set LOGLEVEL=10 in Windows or export LOGLEVEL=10 in Linux and friends.
